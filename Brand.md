# ListItUp Brand

## Positioning

ListItUp is a calm, practical list-making product for people who want to capture thoughts quickly and turn them into useful structure without ceremony.

## Promise

From scattered to sorted, without making organization feel like a second job.

## Personality

- Clear
- Useful
- Fast
- Calm
- Opinionated when it reduces effort

## Voice

Use short, direct language. Prefer verbs that describe the user's real action: capture, organize, complete, archive. Avoid productivity theater, inflated claims, and cute empty states.

## Copy Principles

- Say what the user can do next.
- Prefer concrete labels over abstract system language.
- Keep UI text compact enough for repeated daily use.
- Use encouraging language sparingly and only after real progress.

## Visual Direction

The product should feel focused and quietly polished. Use restrained color, strong hierarchy, high legibility, and dense but comfortable layouts. Avoid marketing-heavy visuals inside the app experience.

## Naming Notes

The product name is `ListItUp`. Use that exact capitalization in prose and UI.
